About

My name is Jordan Beaman. I am a Software that thrives on the personal connections I make from day-to-day. Currently a student at the University of Kansas Coding Bootcamp, set to graduate in January 2019. My previous work experience has helped me be able to collaborate well with others, communicate effectively, and pick up new skills quickly and efficiently. My past work history was mostly centered around customer service, sales, and hospitality, but ultimately, I would love to transition in to a new career as a developer, using the skills I've acquired such as HTML5, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and jQuery. In my free time, I love reading, playing games, watching sports, and traveling! I also like dabbling in Graphic Design on occasion. Feel free to reach out to me any time!
